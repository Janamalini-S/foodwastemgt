About Us

Welcome to FoodShare, a community-driven platform dedicated to reducing food waste and promoting food sharing within local communities. Our mission is to make it easy for people to share surplus food and ensure it reaches those who can use it, rather than going to waste.

Our Mission

At FoodShare, we believe in a sustainable future where food is valued and waste is minimized. By connecting people with surplus food to those who need it, we strive to create a positive impact on the environment and strengthen our communities.

How We Help

Reduce Food Waste

Every post of surplus food helps reduce waste, ensuring that good food doesn’t go to landfill.

Build Community

FoodShare fosters a community of people who care about sharing and sustainability.

Support Those in Need

By sharing surplus food, you can provide valuable support to those who need it most in your area.

Our Impact

Since our launch, we’ve helped divert hundreds of kilos of food from going to waste, supported countless families in need, and built a strong community of like-minded individuals working towards a sustainable future.

Join Us

Become part of FoodShare and help us make a difference. Whether you have surplus food to share or want to benefit from what others are sharing, we welcome you to join our community.
